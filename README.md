# Cosmic Labs

**The intelligence layer for hardware.**

Cosmic Labs builds real-time software that makes physical infrastructure autonomous and observable — think *what observability did for cloud, applied to the hardware layer*. Their platform automates hardware bring-up, configuration, and maintenance across heterogeneous data centers, GPU clusters, HPC environments, and embedded systems, with zero tolerance for latency or failure.

Their flagship product, **Spades**, is a hardware-aware desktop environment and AI copilot for embedded and network engineers — combining real-time device visualization, terminal multiplexing, and AI-driven workflows into a single interface.

---

## What They Build

Cosmic Labs sits at the intersection of physical hardware and software intelligence:

- **Hardware intelligence platform** — real-time systems that discover, configure, monitor, and control physical machines across GPU clusters, data centers, HPC nodes, and edge devices.
- **AI-powered failure prediction** — probabilistic ML models that turn raw system telemetry into calibrated forecasts of hardware failures before they happen.
- **Network intelligence** — automated topology discovery, configuration management, and performance tuning across high-speed fabrics (InfiniBand, RoCE, 400G Ethernet).
- **Edge and embedded AI** — compact, optimized models that run under strict latency, memory, and power constraints on ARM, edge GPUs, radios, and mobile-class silicon.
- **Autonomous operations** — tooling that abstracts away the operational pain of bare-metal provisioning, firmware management, job scheduling, and cross-environment deployments.

Their customers span **defense and intelligence agencies**, **national labs**, **AI compute companies**, **hyperscalers**, **aerospace firms**, and **enterprise data center operators**.
